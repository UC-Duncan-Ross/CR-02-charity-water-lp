About Us

At Charity Water we are dedicated to bringing clean and safe drinking water to people all across the globe. Our mission is to make sure everyone has easy access clear drinking water via wells funded by people like you.

Our Impact

Since 2006 we have been able to complete over 60 projects in areas all across the globe. With your support, we will be able to build more wells in areas where water access may be difficult to obtain. By donating even a dollar you're helping us pay for the resources needed to build a new well for a community in need.

Every dollar counts

With donations from people like you, we can continue to combat the water crisis and bring clean water to those in need. Every dollar you donate goes directly to funding a well to provide clean water for families all accross the globe.
